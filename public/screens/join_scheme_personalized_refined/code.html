Terms and Conditions

By joining this saving scheme, you agree to make consistent monthly payments. The minimum tenure for this gold saving scheme is 11 months to be eligible for the benefit of no making charges on your purchase.

1. Payments must be made before the 10th of every month. Late payments may result in the extension of the maturity date.

2. The gold weight will be credited based on the prevailing market rate at the time of each payment.

3. Upon completion of the 11-month tenure, customers can purchase gold jewelry for the total accumulated value or weight.

4. No cash refunds will be provided. The accumulated amount must be used for jewelry purchase at Annai Jewellers.

5. In case of premature closure, the benefits of the scheme (like zero making charges) will not be applicable, and standard market rates will apply to the purchase.

6. Government taxes, including GST, are applicable at the time of purchase as per prevailing laws.

7. The management reserves the right to alter the terms and conditions of this scheme at its discretion.
